Overview

Purpose

The purpose of the rafting website is to build revenue by introducing the company to the audiences and also introduce the services the company could offer in rafting. Audience will find about the company and also on how to contact the company.

Audience

The target costumers are kids, teens and adolescents with age ranging from 12 to 60 years old.

What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website? They will find a hero picture of people rafting pointing them to the adventure that awaits them. They will find links about the company and how to contact them.

What are their needs that aren't being met? They will not be able to find in the website the detailed itinerary but they will need to contact and speak to a customer respresentative to discuss arrangements for their schedule and so on.

How will my audience usually access my site (desktop, laptop, mobile device)? Audience will access the site on all platforms since audiences will vary greatly in terms of age and their occupation.
